AtCoder Rating Badge

Are you an avid participant in AtCoder contests and want to showcase your rating to the world? Look no further! The AtCoder Rating Badge Generator is here to help you display your AtCoder rating in a stylish and eye-catching way.

Why Use This?

Our badge generator is a simple yet effective tool that allows you to generate a badge displaying your current AtCoder rating. Here's why you should use it:

  1. Personalized Badges: Create a badge that reflects your AtCoder rating accurately.
  2. Easy to Embed: The generated badge can be easily embedded in your blog, website, or online profiles, making it effortless to share your rating with the world.
  3. Automatic Updates: Your badge will automatically update whenever your AtCoder rating changes, ensuring that your profile always displays the latest information.
